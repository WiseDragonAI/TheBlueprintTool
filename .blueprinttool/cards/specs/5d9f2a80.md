The in-app Keys dialog lists the active shortcut contract: `A` opens or focuses the thread panel, `X` starts or stops voice recording, `Esc` cancels capture or clears/closes tooling, `Del` confirms selected object deletion, `Ctrl+C` copies selection, `Ctrl+V` pastes selection, and `Ctrl+D` resizes selected cards to content. The Runbook dialog summarizes workspace launch, card image media behavior, and voice-note configuration.
