Card titles render inline markdown through the shared inline parser while preserving PascalCase wrap opportunities. Leading heading markers such as `##` and `###` are stripped from the displayed title text but stored as title heading level so the title renders with differentiated scale. Entering title edit restores the raw ledger title source so backticks, bold markers, and heading markers are not lost. Each card exposes a small hover/focus delete button in the top-right corner that opens the shared confirmation modal; confirming deletes the card, its connected relationships, and its card thread. Global shortcuts are ignored while card title or description editors own the keyboard event, including `A` for the thread panel.
